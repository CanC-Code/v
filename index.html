MotionForge

Client-side motion transfer. No uploads, no server, runs entirely in your browser via WASM.

1. Model

MotionForge needs two ONNX models (a keypoint detector and a generator) to run motion transfer. It does not ship with weights — you provide a URL to a compatible exported model (see README.md for where to find / how to export one). The model is downloaded once and cached in your browser's IndexedDB; after that it works fully offline.

4. Output

About / Honesty Notes

  • This tool performs motion transfer (warping a still subject according to keypoint motion from a driving video). It does not train a new generative model on your footage.
  • Output quality is bounded by the model you supply — there is no pretrained model bundled with this app.
  • Everything (inference, decoding, encoding) runs in your browser via WebAssembly/WebGPU. No frames or files ever leave your device.
  • This will not produce "perfect realistic" video. Artifacts, warping errors, and identity drift on large motion are expected with current open motion-transfer models.
